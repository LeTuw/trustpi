Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Pi Coin for all payments. Enterprise customers may arrange for alternative payment methods.
Can I change plans anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new rate applies immediately. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the end of your billing cycle.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with access to all Starter plan features. No credit card or Pi payment required.
How do API checks work?
Each time you query our API for a trust score, it counts as one check. Batch operations count as multiple checks based on the number of wallets processed.
